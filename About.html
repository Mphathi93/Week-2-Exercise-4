About the Onwer

Mphathi's Fastfoods was founded by Anelise Sogoni who is known as Mphathi Wo Hlelo. He founded the business at the age of 28 after he decided to resign at his job as a Data Scientist. He then bought a large house container which he towed it back to his home town Mount Frere to start to started a fastfood company called Mphathi's Fastfoods. Mphathi's Fastfoods is a vibrant and customer-centric fast food company that specializes in delivering high-quality, delicious meals with speed and convenience. Known for its wide variety of tasty, freshly prepared options, Mphathi's offers everything from classic burgers and fries to flavorful local dishes, catering to a diverse range of tastes. With a focus on quick service, great value, and a commitment to quality, Mphathi's Fastfoods has become a go-to choice for those seeking a satisfying and affordable meal on the go. Whether you're grabbing a quick bite or enjoying a meal with friends and family, Mphathi's ensures every dining experience is enjoyable and memorable.
